    • Publishing and Citing

    Researchers are encouraged to publish materials with the permission of the Head Archivist. The archives maintains copyright for material that it owns. Fees may apply for certain types of use. Please contact us for more information.

    Material created by the State of New York is in the public domain. Some university records are included in this category. Use cannot legally be restricted for material in the public domain. Please contact the University Archivist for more details.

    Please see our citation guide for information on how to reference materials held here.
